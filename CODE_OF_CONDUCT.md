# Code of Conduct

Voxa is a small, early-stage project. The bar is simple: be respectful, assume good faith, and keep criticism aimed at the code and ideas, not people.

## Expected

- Be direct but civil in issues, PRs, and Discussions.
- Give feedback that's actionable, not just "this is bad."
- Respect that this is a solo-maintained alpha, response times are best-effort.

## Not okay

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language or jokes.
- Spam, self-promotion unrelated to Voxa, or deliberately derailing threads.
- Sharing others' private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Reports go to security@voxa.space (also the address in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)). Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block repeat offenders at their discretion.
